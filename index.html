Crystals Collector

You will be given a new random number between 20 and 120 at the start of each new game.

There are four crystals below. Each crystal hides a random number between 1 and 12. Click on a crystal to add its value to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly generated number. If your score goes above the randomly generated number, you lose.

The crystal values are reset at the beginning of each game.
